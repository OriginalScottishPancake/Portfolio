Beardman Adventures

Beardman Adventures is my very first 2D game made in Unity, and I’m really proud of how it turned out! I used C# along with the Unity Input System to create this fun, cartoon-style side-scrolling shooter. For the characters, I sourced some cool assets from the internet, which helped bring the game to life. The whole experience was a blast, from putting together the quirky characters to figuring out how the gameplay mechanics work. It was a fantastic way for me to learn the ropes of game development, and I’m excited about what I can create next after this awesome project!
