About Me

My projects

I’ve really enjoyed working on my game projects, and I wanted to share a bit about three of my favorites: Vice Noire, Vice Shooter, and Beardman Adventures.

Vice Noire was a chance for me to dive deeper into Unity and explore building immersive gameplay and visuals. It was a fun challenge to combine different elements and really pushed me to learn more about game development.

Vice Shooter came next, and it was my very first project in Unity. I experimented with gameplay mechanics and got hands-on experience with C# scripting. It was a great introduction to bringing game ideas to life, and I learned a lot along the way.

Finally, there’s Beardman Adventures, my first 2D game! This one’s a cartoony side-scrolling shooter where I used characters I found online and applied everything I’d learned so far. It was so rewarding to see the project come together and to create something that felt like a real game.

Each of these projects has taught me so much and has definitely fueled my passion to keep creating and learning in game development!
